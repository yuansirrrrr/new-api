---
title: Subscription Plans
description: Periodic quota packages suitable for users with stable usage needs
---

Subscriptions are periodic packages. After purchase, you enjoy the included quota or privileges for the duration. Click "Subscription" in the left sidebar, or visit `/console/subscription` directly.

![Subscription plans list](/assets/guide/feature-guide/subscription-plans.png)

The subscription list displays all available packages, including package name, price, validity period, and included quota.

Subscription packages offer periodic quota bundles available in daily, weekly, monthly, and other cycles.

## Purchase a Subscription

1. Browse the available plans on the subscription page and review each plan's price, duration, and included quota
2. Click "Purchase" on the plan you want

3. Select a payment method and click "Confirm Payment"
4. After payment, you are redirected back automatically and the subscription status updates to active

## View Current Subscription Status

After purchasing, the top of the subscription page shows your current plan details:

- Plan name and expiration date
- Remaining quota in the plan
- Auto-renewal toggle: set whether to renew automatically when the plan expires

Supports password registration and multiple third-party OAuth one-click logins. First-time users should complete registration first.

## Login

### Password Login

1. Open the platform homepage and click the "Login" button in the top-right corner, or visit `/login` directly

![Login page](/assets/guide/feature-guide/login.png)

2. Enter your username and password, then click "Login"

### Third-Party OAuth Login

To use a third-party account, click the corresponding platform icon at the bottom of the page (GitHub, Discord, LinuxDO, etc.), complete authorization on the third-party page, and you will be logged in automatically

### Forgot Password

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and the system will send a reset link. Click the link to set a new password — the original password becomes invalid immediately.

## Registration

### Registration Process

1. Click the "Register" link on the login page, or visit `/register` directly

![Registration page](/assets/guide/feature-guide/register.png)

2. Enter a username and password
3. Enter your email address and click "Send Code", then enter the verification code you received
4. Click "Register" to complete account creation — you will be redirected to the homepage automatically
